My Mission

I want expand my leadership skills from the physical world into the digital world. I'm currently enrolled in the University of Kansas Full Stack Web Development program. I plan to use the skills I learn at KU to progress towards remote digital management on webdev applications. My supervision style is based on respect, not only per individual, but of the work they are doing. I enjoy being "hands on" and interacting with my personnel through daily tasks, building their confidence and our skills simultaneously. I believe management that invokes fear into the workforce is counter productive not only to the indivuals affected, but to the overall morale of the company. People should enjoy their work and feel accomplishment at the end of their day. I recognize these things and make it my personal misson to earn the respect of my employees and show them the same in return. I hope to find a company that invokes these same ideals into their business practices.
